The Studio Cove

A personal studio brand built by Jacob.

The Studio Cove is my creative workspace for multimedia work. A cove is a sheltered place where rough waters calm down, so the name represents how I bring raw ideas into a focused space and shape them into clean, polished digital work.

It also reflects my personal identity. The word "Cove" is inspired by the inner sound of Jacob, making the brand feel professional while still staying connected to me.
